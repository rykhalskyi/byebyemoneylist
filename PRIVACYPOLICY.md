# Privacy Policy for Bye-Bye Money List
_Last updated: May 10, 2026_

Your privacy is important. Bye-Bye Money List helps you look up product information using barcodes. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## 1. Data Collection
Bye-Bye Money List does **not** automatically collect any personal data.

All information is entered **manually** by the user (e.g., typing a barcode or selecting a product).

The app does not access sensors, contacts, location, or any system data beyond the camera (used **only** for scanning barcodes when you explicitly grant camera permission).

---

## 2. Barcode Lookup via Third‑Party API
When you search for a product by barcode, the app sends the barcode number to a **third‑party product database API** to retrieve product information (e.g., name, brand, category). No personal data is included in these requests.

The third‑party API’s own privacy policy applies to any data they may log (such as the barcode queried and request timestamps). We do not control what they collect.

---

## 3. Data Storage
All data you enter is stored **exclusively on your device** in the current version.

The app does **not** currently:
- send your personal data to any server  
- synchronize data to cloud services  
- share data with third parties  

---

## 4. Future Features (Planned, Not Yet Active)
In later iterations (3rd or 4th) the following features are **under consideration but not yet implemented**:

- **Product upload**: You may be able to upload product information (with barcode) that you saved locally to a server, to help improve the product database.
- **Shopping list sharing**: You may be able to share shopping lists with other users.

If or when these features are implemented, this policy will be updated with full details before they go live.

---

## 5. Data Sharing
Currently, the app does **not** share any data with third parties, except for the barcode lookup described in Section 2.

If sharing features are added in future versions, data will be shared **only when you explicitly choose to do so**.

---

## 6. Third‑Party Services Used
Bye-Bye Money List currently uses:
- A **third‑party product database API** for barcode lookup (Section 2).

The app does **not** use:
- analytics tools  
- advertising networks  
- tracking libraries  
- external links (other than the API calls above)

There are **no ads**, **no payments**, and **no subscriptions**.

---

## 7. Security
Data stored on your device is protected by:
- your device’s operating system  
- your device’s lock screen and security settings  

The app does not transmit or expose your personal data externally beyond barcode lookups (Section 2).

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy
Bye-Bye Money List does not target children and does not collect any data automatically. All data is entered manually by the user.
