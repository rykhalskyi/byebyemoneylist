Your Hierarchy, Your Rules

We don't force a structure on you. Categories are 100% user-defined and hierarchical.

  • Build complex trees (e.g., Food > Dairy > Cheese).
  • Group and track expenses as broadly or granularly as you like.
  • Define custom colors for every category to make charts clear.
