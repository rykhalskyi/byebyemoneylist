Privacy Policy for Bye-Bye Money List

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. Bye-Bye Money List is designed to give you full control over your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

Bye-Bye Money List does not automatically collect any personal data. We do not track your location, access your contacts, or monitor your usage patterns automatically.

All data within the app is entered manually by you (e.g., adding products, prices, or categories). This data is non-personalized and is used solely for the functionality of the app.

2. Data Storage

All data you enter is stored exclusively on your device. We do not maintain any central servers that store your purchase history or lists. Your data remains under your physical control at all times.

3. Data Sharing

The app does not share your data with any third parties unless you explicitly choose to do so (for example, by using the export or share features provided within the app).

4. LLM API & Receipt Scanning

The app includes an optional receipt scanning feature that utilizes Large Language Models (LLM). To use this feature, you must configure an LLM API provider of your choice (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) by providing your own API key.

When you scan a receipt:

  • The image of the receipt is sent directly to the API provider you have configured.
  • We do not save this image. The image is processed in real-time to extract data and is not retained by the app after the scan is complete.
  • The API providers are third-party services. We are not responsible for their data handling practices or privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of whichever provider you choose to use.

5. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear Data" option in the Android system settings for this app.
  • Uninstalling the app from your device.

Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered by us, as we do not have any backups on our servers.
